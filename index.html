About Us

Pork and Poultry Haven is a rapidly growing agribusiness specializing in the production of high-quality poultry and pork products. Founded by Ms. Anna Zayn Phiri, our mission is to provide fresh, healthy, and ethically sourced meats while promoting sustainable farming practices.

We believe in quality, sustainability, and ethical farming. Our poultry and pigs are raised in a free-range environment, ensuring they are well-nurtured, free from harmful additives, and of the highest standard. From whole chickens to value-added products like marinated cuts and sausages, we guarantee freshness and rich flavors in every bite.

As we continue to grow, we remain committed to supporting local farmers, fostering environmentally friendly farming methods, and delivering superior products to households, restaurants, and food businesses. Join us in our journey towards healthier and tastier meals!

Our Services

Whole Chickens

Our whole chickens are raised in a free-range environment, ensuring they are healthy, flavorful, and ethically sourced for the best farm-to-table experience.

Chicken Cuts

We offer a variety of fresh cuts, including breasts, thighs, wings, and drumsticks, perfect for home cooking, grilling, and gourmet meals.

Value-Added Products

Our selection includes marinated chicken breasts, grilled chicken sausages, and crispy chicken burgers—perfect for quick and delicious meals.
